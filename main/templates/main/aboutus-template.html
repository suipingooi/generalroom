About Us

We are a stylish, modern and no-frills shared office premise and facilities. Our space is strategically situated in the heart of bustling Orchard Road, with numerous banking and peripheral office services to support your business.

Our Vision

To become a sought-after collaborative business base. A place where many things happen: from business deals to networking, from a friendly conversation to a spark of business insights and ideas.

Our Story

Our name is derived from the inspiration of an army General who is stern, yet effective, as well as a play on the word 'General' to provide a wide-ranging space for the general needs of businesses.

Our Logo

A badge is a recognition awarded to officials with exceptional accomplishment, contributon or qualification. This simple yet sleep representation of the badge and plagie given to men of honour for their service portrays The General Room's aspirations and achievements.
